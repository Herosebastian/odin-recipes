Goulash Recipe

Description

Goulash is allegedly a comfort meal using pantry staples always being available, and I am now begnning to realize comfort food might be food that you can have all the time/always have access to

Its interesting because Goulash sounds like its supposed to be gross but looks good, I also had for some reason assumed its of Russian origin but its actually Hungarian

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Yellow Onion
  • Garlic
  • Water
  • Canned Tomato Sauce
  • Canned Diced Tomato
  • Soy Sauce?
  • Italian Herb Seasoning
  • Salt
  • Bay Leaves
  • Elboy Macaroni

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients and prep and dice vegatables
  2. Heat Olive oil in an oven to medium-high heat. Add the onions and cook for 3 minutes stirring regularly
  3. Add ground beef and cook, breaking up the meat for an even brown that takes ~5 minutes. Add garlic and cook for 3 minutes stirring regularly
  4. Stir in water, tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, soy sauce, italian seasoning, salt, and bay leaves. Bring to a simmer and let cook for 5 minutes
  5. Stir in the uncooked pasta, simmer over medium heat until pasta is tender. 11-13 minutes
  6. Discard bay leaves and serve
