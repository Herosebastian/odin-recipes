Potato Soup Recipe

Description

Potato soup combines the creamy cheesiness of a regular cheese soup with the delectable savory salty taste of potato soup

I am not really sure why I enjoy this dish as much as I do but I theorize its fueled by nostalgia from eating it in highschool from the Safeway I visited every morning

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Chicken Broth
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Cream Cheese
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Green Onions

Directions

  1. Combine potatoes, ham, onions, celery, chicken broth, salt, pepper, and garlic powder in a slow cooker, regularly stir to combine and prevent inconsistencies
  2. Cook on low heat until the celery is soft and cooked, usually about 4-5 hours
  3. Mix in the cream cheese and cheddar cheese. Stir until both cheeses are melted and combined, cook on low for 30 minutes
  4. Serve and garnish with green onion
