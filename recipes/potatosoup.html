Potato Soup Recipe

Description

Potato soup combines the creamy cheesiness of a regular cheese soup with the delectable savory salty taste of potato soup

I am not really sure why I enjoy this dish as much as I do but I theorize its fueled by nostalgia from eating it in highschool from the Safeway I visited every morning

Ingredients

Potatoes

Onion

Celery

Chicken Broth

Salt

Pepper

Garlic Powder

Cream Cheese

Cheddar Cheese

Green Onions

Directions

Combine potatoes, ham, onions, celery, chicken broth, salt, pepper, and garlic powder in a slow cooker, regularly stir to combine and prevent inconsistencies

Cook on low heat until the celery is soft and cooked, usually about 4-5 hours

Mix in the cream cheese and cheddar cheese. Stir until both cheeses are melted and combined, cook on low for 30 minutes

Serve and garnish with green onion
