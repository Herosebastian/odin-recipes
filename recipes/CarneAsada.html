Carne Asada Recipe

Description

Carne Asada is a traditional mexican dish often served inside tacos or burrtios alongside several other topings like guacamole, salsa, and mixed vegetables

This dish is often served at gatherings and parties

Ingredients

skirt steak

limes

vinegar

garlic

onion

cilantro

jalapeno

olive oil

salt

black pepper

cumin

oregano

Steps

    First clean and prepare all the ingredients

    Make the simple marianade

    Marinate the meat nad let sit for minimum 1 hour preferred 4-8 hours

    Once done marinating grill the carne asada, I prefer medium well so let it cook for about 7 minutes on each side

    Then let it rest for a few minutes and its not ready to serve
