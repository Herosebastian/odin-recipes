Carne Asada Recipe

Description

Carne Asada is a traditional mexican dish often served inside tacos or burrtios alongside several other topings like guacamole, salsa, and mixed vegetables

This dish is often served at gatherings and parties

Ingredients

  • skirt steak
  • limes
  • vinegar
  • garlic
  • onion
  • cilantro
  • jalapeno
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • cumin
  • oregano

Steps

  1. First clean and prepare all the ingredients
  2. Make the simple marianade
  3. Marinate the meat nad let sit for minimum 1 hour preferred 4-8 hours
  4. Once done marinating grill the carne asada, I prefer medium well so let it cook for about 7 minutes on each side
  5. Then let it rest for a few minutes and its not ready to serve
